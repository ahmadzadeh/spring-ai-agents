# Supplier Initial Assessment Skill

## Profiling guidelines

- Be factual and objective. Do not assign risk scores here.
- For each field, note the source URL where the information came from.
- If a field has no evidence, write "Not found" — do not invent or guess.

## Certification verification levels

- **Verified**: Certificate shown on official certifying body's registry (e.g. BSI, Bureau Veritas, LRQA)
- **Claimed**: Mentioned on supplier's own website or marketing materials only
- **Unverifiable**: Referenced but no source URL or registry found

## Evidence completeness scoring

Assign a completeness score out of 10 based on:
- 2 pts: Company identity fully confirmed (legal name, reg number, country)
- 2 pts: Products/services clearly described with use cases
- 2 pts: At least one certification verified (not just claimed)
- 2 pts: No adverse information found, OR adverse info found and documented
- 2 pts: Cybersecurity posture documented (positive or negative)

## Red flag triggers

Always flag the following as red flags:
- No official website found
- Website has no SSL certificate
- No presence on any professional network
- Certifications claimed but not traceable to issuing body
- News articles about regulatory fines, data breaches, or insolvency (last 3 years)
- Registered address does not match operational address
